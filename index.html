What it is

The term updog is a way to joke around with pals as this would substitute the slang word of "dawg"
to the term dog. This would lead for jokes about the animal dog to try and trick the person that it is used against.
However, this joke is very predicatable and not funny to a lot of people, but it is one of the most classic jokes every made and served as the base of others.

How it is used

An example of using the joke "updog" would be:
"Does it not smell like updog in here?"
"What is updog?"
"Not much, how about you."
This shows how it can be used in jokes as this is what updog told me. What is updog you say? Not much, how about you? :) hehe
